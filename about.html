About US






Our Company

Crazy Burger was founded in 1885 in San Francisco, California. Our family run business has continued our passion to pursue the craziest and wackiest burgers imaginable. We source our fresh and natural ingredients from local suppliers and from other worlds. Though we cannot disclose the methods we use to gather ingredients from other worlds, we can declare that our products are unparalleled in power, passion, and prestige. No other company can copy what we offer. We hope you continue to follow us through our journey into the future into discovering even more crazier burgers!











Our Values

Ingenuity


We strive for innovation and evolution. Our food will never stop growing in its potential and awesomeness. We devote ourselves to exploring the unknown in the world of burgers and powers.

Impact


We strive to create unforgettable experiences. Our food will never stop changing our customers' lives. We devote ourselves to forging the wildest and wackiest burgers with tremendous consequences.
