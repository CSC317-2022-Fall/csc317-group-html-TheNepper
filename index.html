Crazy Burger

Welcome to Crazy Burger!

Each burger comes with two 1/4 lb juicy patties - cooked medium and served on a freshly baked bun with lettuce, tomato, red onion, and Crazy Sauce!
Each burger grants a unique power upon consumption that lasts 1 hour.

Here is our menu.

Featherfall

- Fall slowly -
Force of gravity is reduced while falling. Fall almost 3x slower.

Gills

- Breathe fishly -
Breathe underwater, but cannot breathe normal air.

Gravity

- Reverse gravity -
Fall towards space. Staying indoors is recommended.

Invisible

- Vanish -
Clothing can still be seen. Be naked if full invisibility is preferred.

Nightowl

- Night vision -
See better in the dark, worse in the light. Eyes are light sensitive.

Teleport

- Random Warp -
Teleport to a random location on Earth every minute.

Animal

- Animal speech -
Communicate with animals, but not humans.

Telepathy

- Speak mentally -
Communicate with people through the mind, but become mute.

Sticky

- Glue -
Become insanely sticky. Only affects the skin.

Bouncy

- Ball -
Become insanely bouncy. Only affects the skin.

Hallucinate

- See things -
See, hear, touch, smell, taste things that do not exist.

Wild

- Mystery -
Gain a random effect from the other burgers. Good luck.
