FAQ

Q: Can I share my burger with other people to share the powers?

    A: No, only the first person can be affected with each burger.


Q: Can I eat the burger later to gain its effects?

    A: No, the effects will disappear after 1 hour of it being cooked.


Q: What happens if I eat the same type of burger?

    A: The duration of the effect will refresh and last 1 hour again.


Q: What happens if I eat different types of burgers?

    A: The effects will overlap and multiple effects will activate.


Q: Is this safe to eat?

    A: No! You must be crazy to even consider eating these burgers!


Q: Will there be more Crazy Burger locations around the world?

    A: Currently, it is a work in progress to have more franchises worldwide!
