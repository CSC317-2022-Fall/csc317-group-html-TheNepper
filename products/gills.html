Gills Burger

The Gills Burger grants the power of breathing like a fish. The person is able to breathe underwater with no problems while remaining out of water eventually leads to suffocation. This is extremely dangerous.

The meat is obtained from a kraken. It is extremely moist and juice does not seem to stop oozing from the burger.
