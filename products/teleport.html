Teleport Burger

The Teleport Burger grants the power of random teleportation. The person will teleport to a random surface level location on Earth every minute. The only valid teleportation locations are ones with enough space to fit the person and are reachable through normal human navigation. Obstacles such as doors, walls, and windows will block the path to teleport. This is extremely dangerous.

The meat is enchanted by a magic spell. It seems to tremble violently.
